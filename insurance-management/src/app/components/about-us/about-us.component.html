About Us CYA22KA Holdings

CYA22KA Holdings is a pioneering, 100% female-owned South African company specializing in a central database for deceased policyholders and pre-deceased beneficiary notifications.

Our Mission

We provide peace of mind and financial security by ensuring policyholders are informed when beneficiaries predecease them, and by maintaining a reliable central database.

Nature of Business

We support insurers with accurate deceased policyholder data and proactive notifications to help process claims efficiently.

Problem Statement

With over R88.56 billion in unclaimed assets in South Africa, our system bridges the gap by tracking and notifying relevant parties, ensuring insurance legacies are honored.

Our Services

  • Central database for deceased policyholders
  • Pre-deceased beneficiary alerts
  • Funeral and life policy lookup
